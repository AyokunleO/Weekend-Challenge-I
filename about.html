ABOUT ME

Maintenance management and enlisting latest technology to solve construction and maintenance related issues is what Ayokunle does. He is passionate about the development of the construction industry in his country; Nigeria. In 2016, he developed a Framework for Building maintenance management of University Hostel facilities in Nigeria using Ondo state as a case study.
